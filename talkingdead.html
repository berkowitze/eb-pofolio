Zombies for Good

summary

My team designed and built a virtual-reality game to help people deal with public speaking anxiety. In the game, players tell improvized stories to an audience of approaching zombies, which are remotely controlled by a live Twitch audience.

project

Twitch is a live-streaming video platform with over 15 million daily users. We leveraged the Twitch platform to create a game that allows live audience participation.
